The World of Vienna

Important Locations

Location Image Information
Schönbrunn Palace

It is a major palace and its gardens have been fantastic for over 300 years. It is a sight all must go to see when they go to Vienna.
Strandbad Gänsehäufel

It is a very nice area with beautiful scenery. It is one of the most famous beaches with tennis courts, pools, and a lake!
Vienna State Opera

A very fantastic opera house. It is a spectacle to look at and it houses famous performances that people have loved worldwide.
St. Peter's Catholic Church

The church is a beautiful domed church. It is admired for its music and traditional look/feel.
Kahlenberg

Kahlenberg has a fantastic view. The view consists of nice greenery coming from the grass and the trees. You are in a good position to see a lot of Vienna.
Imperial Crypt

This crypt houses historical objects and coffins. It takes around 30 minutes to complete but once you exit you will realize how much you learned.
Raxalpe West Plateau Circuit

This is a nice hiking trail on mountains. There is a great view and the scenery is filled with green.
Steirereck

This restaurant is a once in a lifetime experience. It has great service and good food. It's design blows people away and it is definitely a place to go to!

Travel

The path from New York to Vienna is very long


In order to go from New York to Vienna you have to take an 8 hour flight, costing as low as $1,057. An 8 hour flight is a direct flight, and the flight can be 11+ hours if it is a connecting flight.
